Return Policy

Our return policy covers only received defective and damaged products of online purchases. We want to ensure you have a smooth experience, so please follow these guidelines:

Important: Video Proof Required

The customer must take a video of unboxing as proof for the replacement. This helps us verify the condition of the product at the time of delivery.

  • Time Limit: The customer is entitled to return the item within 48 hours of receiving the product.
  • Condition: To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Original Packaging: The item must be in the original packaging with all accessories included.
  • Proof of Purchase: To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

Refund Policy

If the product sold by WorthyTen is found to be faulty or develops a fault within the warranty period:

  • First Step: We endeavor to repair the product.
  • If Repair is Not Possible: We will offer a replacement/refund provided the fault is covered under warranty.
  • Refund Amount: The refund processed would be to current or original value (whichever is lower) as per company policy and condition of the item at return.

Note

Refunds are processed based on the condition of the returned item and company policy. Ensure the product is in its original condition with all accessories for the best outcome.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Here's our cancellation policy:

  • Within 24 Hours: You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. A 5% cancellation fee will be charged.
  • After 24 Hours: Order cancellation is not available after 24 hours of placing an order.

Why the 5% Fee?

This covers payment processing fees and administrative costs associated with order preparation.

Shipping Information

  • Shipping Time: Minimum 3 to 7 working days for delivery.
  • Free Shipping: Available for orders above ₹500.
  • Tracking: Track your order status through our platform.
