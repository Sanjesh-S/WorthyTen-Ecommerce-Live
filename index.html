Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know about selling with WorthyTen

How is the price calculated?
Your base price is derived from recent market data and current demand. As you answer assessment questions, we apply transparent adjustments for condition, functionality, and included accessories. Our AI-powered pricing engine ensures you get the best market value.
How does pickup and payment work?
We schedule a free pickup at your convenience - home or office. Our expert inspects the device to verify the condition. Once verified, we transfer the amount instantly to your bank account or UPI within minutes. Same day, guaranteed.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely! We follow industry-standard security protocols. Your personal information is encrypted and never sold to third parties. All devices are factory-reset during inspection if requested. Your privacy is our priority.
What if I'm not happy with the final price?
No worries! If the physical inspection reveals any discrepancies from your assessment, we'll explain the revised price. You're free to accept or decline - no pressure, no fees. We only proceed when you're satisfied.
Which devices do you accept?
We accept DSLR cameras, lenses, smartphones, laptops, iPads, and tablets from major brands including Canon, Nikon, Sony, Fujifilm, Apple, Samsung, and more. Check our catalog for your specific model - over 700+ models supported!
