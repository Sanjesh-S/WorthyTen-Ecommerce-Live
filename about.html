Our Story

WorthyTen, established in Tamil Nadu, India, in 2022 as WorthyTen Recommerce Pvt Ltd, specializes in a wide range of cameras, including DSLR and SLR cameras, action cameras, lenses, smartphones and laptops. The company focuses on offering pre-owned, lightly used, and refurbished products, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both buyers and sellers.

Committed to providing the best prices possible, WorthyTen aims to create a safe environment for electronics transactions. To enhance customer satisfaction, we also offer free coupons, gift cards, delivery, and pickup services.

Mr. Aravindhan Sakthivel

Founder & CEO

Aravindhan launched WorthyTen Recommerce Pvt. Ltd. in March 2022 with a mission to establish a trustworthy marketplace that meets the needs of consumers and supports a sustainable economy. His vision extends beyond commerce — it aims to foster a marketplace where enthusiasts of cameras, smartphones, laptops, and other gadgets can buy and sell with confidence.

Our Vision

One of our key aims is to inspire sustainable practices by championing the responsible recycling and reuse of electronic gadgets. Through WorthyTen, we envision a thriving ecosystem where technology lovers can not only buy and sell devices but also play a vital role in reducing electronic waste, paving the way for a more sustainable future.

The success of our initial showrooms marked the beginning of WorthyTen's venture to establish a nationwide presence. Under Aravindhan Sakthivel's visionary leadership, the goal is to create branches across the country, forming a network where people from various parts of India can visit, interact, and engage with the vibrant community of camera and electronic gadget enthusiasts.
