Warranty Coverage

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware malfunctions
  • Software faults (normal use)
  • Functional failures

What's NOT Covered

  • Physical damage
  • Water/Liquid damage
  • Fire/Electrical damage
  • Rooting/Jailbreaking damage
  • Customer self-repair attempts

Accessories & Consumables

The warranty does NOT cover accessories and consumables, including:

  • Bag, Strap, USB Cable, AV Cable
  • Lens Cap, Battery Leakage
  • Flash Tube, Display Vignette
  • Lens Fungus, Dust

Exception for Camera Accessories

Battery and Charger will be provided with a one-time replacement warranty within 7 days for cameras only.

Terms & Conditions

  • The warranty is valid only for the specific device and the original customer. It is not transferable.
  • If the product is found to be faulty within the warranty period, we first endeavor to repair the product.
  • If repair is not possible, we will offer a 1000-day valid voucher (to current or original value, whichever is lower).
  • Warranty service is valid only at WorthyTen authorized service centers. Shipping charges may apply.
  • Warranty confirmation is done through Serial Number and our tracing system.
  • No cash/card refund shall be made under any circumstances.
  • Warranty does not cover replaced products.
  • Customer must provide Proof of Purchase (Invoice) including Warranty Card to avail warranty.

Repair Timeline

In WorthyTen warranty period, repairs can take up to 90 working days. For support, call 9843010746 (10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday-Friday). We always endeavor to get your product repaired within a reasonable timeframe. For brand warranty period, repair timing will be decided by the brand service center only.

Additional Notes

  • Software issues unrelated to the repair and tampering with internal hardware are not included within warranty.
  • Mishandling of product may not be covered (e.g., keeping battery in product for long time when not in use).
  • WorthyTen is not responsible for any loss of data occurring as a result of repair. Customers are advised to back up all data prior to repair.
  • All prices are inclusive of GST.
  • Tax invoice for second-hand goods sold - buyers shall not be eligible to claim input tax credit thereon.
  • All disputes are covered under Coimbatore jurisdiction only.
  • WorthyTen reserves the right to modify or update warranty terms at any time without prior notice.
